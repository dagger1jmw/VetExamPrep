NORMAL VALUES (IMPORTANT FOR EXAMS)
🐰 Rabbit
• Temp: 38.5–40°C
• HR: 130–325
• RR: 32–60
• lifespan: 6–13 years
🐀 Rat
• Temp: 37.2–38.3°C
• HR: 250–450
• RR: 70–115
• lifespan: 2–3 years
🐹 Guinea pig
• Temp: 38.3–40°C
• HR: 230–320
• lifespan: 4–8 years
🐹 Hamster
• Temp: 37–38°C
• HR: 310–470
• lifespan: 1.5–3 years

MEMORY TIP
• 👉 No eating = emergency

🐭 Chinchilla
• Temp: ~38°C
• HR: 100–150
• lifespan: up to 15 years
