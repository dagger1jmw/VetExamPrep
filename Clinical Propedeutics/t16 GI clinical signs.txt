Gases

• gagging : is an involuntary retching reflex stimulated by mechanical irritation of the posterior palate or pharynx
-> pharingitis, bronchitis, foreign body…

-> eructation = expulsion of gastrointestinal gas from the oral cavity
-> flatulence : expulsion of gas from the anorectal canal
-> borborygmus : term used to describe the rumbling noise of gas as it is propagated
through the gastrointestinal tract
=> all three can be normal manifestations of gastrointestinal tract function, but may
be presented as problems because of frequency, intensity, or odor
