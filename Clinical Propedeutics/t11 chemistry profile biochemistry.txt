NON-PATHOLOGIC vs PATHOLOGIC MURMURS
Non-pathologic
• soft (grade 1–2)
• systolic
• short duration
• disappear at rest
• no other abnormalities
Pathologic
• loud (grade 3–6)
• continuous or diastolic
• radiates
• present all the time
• associated abnormalities
MEMORY TIP
• Loud + persistent = pathologic
